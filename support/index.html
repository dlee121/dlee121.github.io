Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove ads?

Tap the "Remove Ads" button on the home screen or game type screen, then complete the one-time purchase through the App Store or Google Play. You can also tap "Restore Purchases" if you've already bought it on another device.

How do I save my scores to the leaderboard?

Create a free account by tapping Login on the home screen. Once signed in, your scores are automatically submitted to the global leaderboard.

I purchased "Remove Ads" but ads are still showing.

Try tapping "Restore Purchases" in the Remove Ads dialog. If that doesn't work, restart the app. If the issue persists, contact us at the email above.

Is the app free to play?

Yes! Number Blitz is completely free. The optional "Remove Ads" purchase simply removes banner and interstitial ads for a cleaner experience.
